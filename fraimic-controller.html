Send images to any specific frame — no tapping required

1 Install the Bookmarklet

Drag the button below into your browser's bookmarks bar. You only need to do this once.

Drag this to your bookmarks bar ↓

🖼️ Fraimic Controller
← drag me to the bookmarks bar →

If your bookmarks bar isn't visible, press ⌘ Shift B (Mac) or Ctrl Shift B (Windows) to show it.

2 Use the Controller

Each time you want to send an image to a specific frame:

  • Go to app.fraimic.com in Chrome (you need to be logged in)
  • Click 🖼️ Fraimic Controller in your bookmarks bar
  • A panel will appear — select the destination frame
  • Drop or choose your image
  • Click Send to Selected Frame
💡 Tip: You can rename your frames from the controller panel so they're easier to identify (e.g. "Living Room", "Bedroom"). Names are saved to your Fraimic account.

✦ What It Does

  • Lists all your frames with battery level and last-seen time
  • Lets you name each frame for easy identification
  • Sends images directly to the frame you choose — no tapping required
  • Supports drag-and-drop or file picker
  • Works with JPG, PNG, and WebP
  • Click the bookmark again to close the panel
